The Elder Scrolls: Skyrim

Skyrim, the triple A game from Bethesda games studios is one that is near and dear to my heart. Coming out after the beloved Oblivion, Bethesda really stepped
it up a few nothes with this one. This RPG is a memorable one. From the seemingly endless landscape to the multitude of storylines, you most certainly will find
something you will love here. Not to mention the dragons, charater progression, differnet combat styles, magic, more mythical creatures than I can name here.
One of the most memorable things for me would be the soundtrack, more specifically the title screen song. It truly gives me goosbumps everytime. To Bethesda's Skyrim

My Experince With the Game

I have put plenty of hours into the game on my ps4. A majority of it into my dagger wielding, sneak thief, khajiit. I have gotten every achievement there is with
him. He can easyily take on any foe that crosses his path being able to go completely invisable to come up behind them and stabbing them in the back. Fights do
do not last long here. I have ventured into every skill there is and must say playing with just my daggers can be a struggle early on. Most certainly one of the
more difficult play styles to get going. This however is very satisfing to take out an entire camp from the shadows.
