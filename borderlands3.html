Borderlands 3

Borderlands 3 is quite possibly the ultimate looter shooter that is out there. You can spend hours upon hours farming a boss and get a different gun everytime.
This on top of the four different classes you are able to play create tons of combinations for whatever you may be feeling. You can throw an infinite amount
of grenades, one shot powerful bosses, even become almost invincible. Even if you get bored of playing a certain way it is incredibly simple to just respec
your skill points and get a couple new guns that synergize. Also reaching the maximum potential of a bulid can give you quite the satisfaction. To gearbox games

Zane

My go to character would have to be Zane the operator. I have about a total of 6 whole days played on my one Zane alone. One of the best builds in the game allows
you to keep 100% up time on your action skills. With the way his skills are creates a significant power spike allowing you to shred through any foe that dares
to cross your path. Since the release there has only been three different skill trees for zane, but with an upcoming dlc, there is going to be another one.
Right now we can only speculate about how good it may be, but if it is anything like his others, we are bound to have a great time.
