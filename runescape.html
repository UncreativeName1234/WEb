Oldschool Runescape

This classic game is one I have only recently gotten into. Mostly because it is mainly a computer game. They have made a mobile version but I believe
to truly experience the game to the fullest you should play on PC. There is a lot of stuff to do here all of it with the goal to make your account better.
The main way i have been going about it is to unlock a variety of different teleports because the map is quite large. You actually can't run across the
entire thing, you have to take a boat or two. Run energy is not unlimited either. Which leads me to the skills.

Official OSRS site

Skilling

The main portion of the game is the different skills that you can train. They are quite the time sink even when getting the max exp possible. Your time
put into these skills will not go unrewarded however. On your way to any 99, you unlock new items, better skilling methods, new areas, and the most prestigious
is the cape you are able to get once you reach 99. It's really the ultimate flex, these capes give you special perks as well as an exclusive emote for ultimate
show-boating.
